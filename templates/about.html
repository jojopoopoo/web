Our Method

Our python script takes any news headline keywords and crawls over Twitter, then outputs the parameters of any Twitter user tweeting those news, their follower counts, and the number times retweeted. By incorporating Snopes.com, a dedicated website to verifying online stories, the goal of the project is help users to map out the social media footprint of Twitter news.
